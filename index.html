5g advance

5G technology offers significant advancements in terms of bandwidth capacity, transmission speeds, and lower latency, enabling higher data transmission speeds and exciting possibilities for new developments and applications in numerous industries and economic sectors. The advancements in 5G technology can amplify the bandwidth by 1000 times, and all the networks around the world can have worldwide coverage. The advancements to 5G technology include network resource functions, cloud technologies, and expansion of IoT capabilities. 5G-Advanced offers key enhancements such as XR (AR, VR, gaming), enhanced coverage and MIMO performance, 5G to replace GSM-R, evolution beyond smartphone, and accurate location services. 5G will expand the mobile ecosystem to new industries, contributing to cutting-edge user experiences such as boundless extreme reality (XR).
